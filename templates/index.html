Why We Verify Every Agent

Protection Against Fraud

Unverified agents can disappear with your deposit. Our EARB verification ensures agents are licensed, traceable, and accountable to professional standards.

Legal Compliance (POCAMLA)

Under Kenyan law, all real estate agents must be registered. We enforce this to protect you from illegal operators and ensure all transactions comply with anti-money laundering regulations.

Quality Assurance

Verified agents maintain professional standards, provide accurate property information, and handle disputes according to EARB ethical guidelines.
