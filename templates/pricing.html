Common Questions
About payments and listings
Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed via Paystack's official secure gateway with 256-bit encryption. LinkPoint never stores your card details or PINs.
What happens after I pay?
Your listing is activated immediately after payment confirmation from Paystack. You'll receive an email confirming your plan is live.
Can I get a refund?
All fees are one-time and non-refundable once the listing is activated. If you experience a technical issue, contact us within 24 hours at linkpoint-hub@gmail.com.
How long does my listing stay active?
Tenant search listings are active for 30 days. Rental listings run 60 days. Sales and commercial listings get 90 days of premium visibility.
What payment methods are supported?
We support All major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) and Mobile Money (M-Pesa) via Paystack's secure checkout.
